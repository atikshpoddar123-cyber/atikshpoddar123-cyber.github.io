How To Play Colony Command

Fast loop: capture, farm, train, repeat. Keep your streak alive for bonus drops.
1. Capture
Click a nearby tile and press E to claim it.
2. Snowball Economy
Use 2 Farm and 3 Market early.
3. Train Units
Build Barracks/Archery, then use Q / R.
4. Keep Momentum
Do actions quickly to stack streak rewards.
Arcade tip: fast actions are stronger than perfect actions.
Press H anytime to open/close this tutorial.
